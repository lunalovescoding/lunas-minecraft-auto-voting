About

Auto-Vote Extension helps you automatically vote for your Minecraft servers on various listing sites.

Supported Sites

  • minecraft-mp.com
  • minecraftservers.org
  • minecraft-server-list.com
  • minecraft-server.net

How to Use

  1. Add your server by going to the "Add Project" tab
  2. Enter your server name, username, and voting URL
  3. The extension will automatically vote when you visit voting pages
  4. You can also manually trigger votes from the popup

Privacy

All your data is stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or send any information to external servers.
